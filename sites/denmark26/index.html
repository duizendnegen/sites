Where to Stay

All options for 2 people sharing. Book early — May is early season and the island is small. Bikes can be pre-booked to meet you at Sælvig harbour.

The
Itinerary

Berlin to Middelfart — arrival by train and ferry, three nights on the island, departure south to Middelfart.

10
Saturday · Arrival

Berlin → Samsø

Depart Berlin Hbf early (~06:00–07:00) by ICE/IC toward Hamburg, then Hamburg → Aarhus by train (DSB / Interrail).

Aarhus → Sælvig harbour by fast ferry (Samsø Rederi express from Dokk1 pier, ~1h crossing). Ferries run roughly every 2h, 06:00–20:00; book in advance at tilsamsoe.dk.

Pick up pre-booked bikes at Sælvig harbour (Ø-Hjul e-bikes or Samsø Cykler). Bike to accommodation (~8–15km). Evening: settle in, dinner at or near accommodation.

13
Tuesday · Departure

Morning Hike → Middelfart

Light morning activity: beach walk, Besser Rev coastal path, or Brattingsborg forest trail.

Ferry from Sælvig → Hou (Jutland) or Ballen → Kalundborg (Zealand), then train connection south to Middelfart. Arrive by evening.
