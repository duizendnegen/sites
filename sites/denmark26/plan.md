# Samsø Trip — May 10–13, 2025

> Berlin → Samsø → Middelfart  
> 2 travelers · Public transport · Bikes on island · Outdoor focus

---

## Itinerary

### Day 1 — Saturday May 10: Berlin → Samsø
- Depart Berlin Hbf early (~06:00–07:00) by ICE/IC toward Hamburg
- Hamburg → Aarhus by train (via DSB / interrail)
- Aarhus → Sælvig harbour by fast ferry (Samsø Rederi express from Dokk1, Aarhus harbour; ~1h crossing)
  - Ferry runs roughly every 2h, 06:00–20:00; book in advance at tilsamsoe.dk
- Pick up pre-booked bikes at Sælvig harbour (Ø-Hjul e-bikes or Samsø Cykler regular/e-bikes)
- Bike to accommodation (~8–15km depending on choice)
- Evening: settle in, dinner at or near accommodation

### Day 2 — Sunday May 11: North Island — Nordby Bakker & Issehoved
- Full hiking day in Nordby Bakker
- **Route option A (18km loop):** Nordby → Nordby Church → Møgelskår → west coast cliffs → Ballebjerg (64m, highest point, lookout tower) → Pelkeshøj (27m, panoramic views) → Issehoved (northernmost tip) → east coast beach → Nordby
- **Route option B (11km loop):** Ballebjerg → Issehoved → east coast beach → Nordby (accessible, few short steep climbs)
- Lunch on the hills or at Nordby village
- Evening: dinner at Nordby Kro restaurant or Nordby 13 (local brasserie, farm-to-table)

### Day 3 — Monday May 12: Cycling the Island + Stavns Fjord
- Full cycling day across the island
- Suggested loop: Sælvig → Havvejen north → Langør (Stavns Fjord views, birdlife) → Nordby Hede heath → back south via Onsbjerg → Tranebjerg (main town) → Ballen harbour
- Stops: farm roadside stalls for local produce (asparagus season in May), Samsø Madsnedkeri (old barn restaurant, seasonal local menu), Ballen harbour for smoked fish at Røgeriet
- Optional detour: Sams Island Distillery (gin, rum, local spirits) in Brundby
- Evening: Ballen harbour for sunset drinks; dinner at Restaurant Dokken (classic Danish, sea views) or Røgeriet

### Day 4 — Tuesday May 13: Morning hike or ride → Middelfart
- Light morning activity (beach walk, Besser Rev coastal path, or Brattingsborg forest trail)
- Ferry from Sælvig → Hou (Jutland) or Ballen → Kalundborg (Zealand)
  - Hou route: train south through Jutland → cross to Funen → Middelfart
  - Kalundborg route: train through Zealand/Funen → Middelfart
- Arrive Middelfart by evening of May 13

## Accommodation

> All options are for 2 people sharing. Book early — May is early season and the island is small.  
> Bikes can be pre-booked to meet you at Sælvig harbour (Ø-Hjul or Samsø Cykler).

### Option 1 — Nordby Kro (North Island Inn)
- **Type:** B&B / Inn
- **Location:** Nordby village, north island
- **Distance from Sælvig harbour:** ~10km by bike (flat)
- **Distance to hiking:** Best access — Ballebjerg 25-min walk, Issehoved 4.8km
- **Rooms:** 14 rooms, all with private bathroom, some with balcony/terrace
- **Includes:** Breakfast (buffet with local produce); on-site restaurant and bar
- **Food:** Restaurant focuses on local seasonal ingredients; good wine list and draft beer
- **Vibe:** Classic Danish kro — warm, personal, village-centre location
- **Book:** nordbykro.dk

### Option 2 — Rum og Rooms (Central Island Guesthouse)
- **Type:** Guesthouse / small hotel
- **Location:** Brundby, mid-island
- **Distance from Sælvig harbour:** ~8km by bike
- **Rooms:** 9 rooms, mix of private and shared bathrooms; robes provided for shared-bath rooms
- **Includes:** Breakfast (homemade buns, rye bread, local apple juice, eggs, cheese, fruit) at DKK 95/person; bar open on return
- **Food:** Communal dinners during high season (check availability for May); local, organic, Samsø-sourced; vegetarian/vegan on request
- **Vibe:** Communal, food-forward, farmhouse feel — guests eat together
- **Note:** May is outside peak individual booking season (July–August); email to confirm availability
- **Book:** rumogrooms.dk — book direct (info@rumogrooms.dk)

### Option 3 — Sælvigbugtens Camping Glamping
- **Type:** Glamping tent
- **Location:** Sælvig Bay, mid-west island — right next to ferry harbour
- **Distance from Sælvig harbour:** Walking distance
- **Setup:** 19m² Lotus Belle tent on wooden platform, box spring mattresses, duvets/pillows, gas grill, fridge, electric kettle, kitchenware, private terrace
- **Facilities:** Shared showers and toilets in campsite building (note: road between tents and beach creates some noise)
- **Price:** From DKK 680–835/night for 2
- **Vibe:** Most outdoorsy option; wake up to Kattegat — but 10km from north-island hiking
- **Book:** saelvigbugtens-camping.dk

### Option 4 — Yduns Have Organic Farm Cabins
- **Type:** Wooden cabin on working farm
- **Location:** Central island, near Stavns Fjord
- **Distance from Sælvig harbour:** ~8km by bike
- **Setup:** Small primitive wooden cabin for 2–4 people; shared kitchen, dining area, bathroom and lounge in farmhouse; private terrace on larger cabins; 2km to beach
- **Food:** No meals served, but farm shop on site sells seasonal organic vegetables, fruit and herbs directly; fully equipped shared kitchen
- **Vibe:** Rustic, rural, working organic farm — over 100 varieties of vegetables/herbs grown on site; ideal for self-catering with exceptional local produce
- **Note:** Charming but genuinely primitive — shared facilities
- **Book:** direct at ydunshavesamsø.dk (recommended over Airbnb to avoid fees)

### Option 5 — Vesborg Fyr (Lighthouse Apartments)
- **Type:** Holiday apartment in converted lighthouse complex
- **Location:** South-western tip of island
- **Distance from Sælvig harbour:** ~15km by bike (longest transfer)
- **Setup:** Renovated apartments in former lighthouse keeper's buildings; sea views on all sides; 50m to water; private garden/terrace
- **Price:** Via Novasol; check vesborgfyr.dk for current rates
- **Vibe:** Most dramatic setting — 1858 lighthouse, panoramic views over Samsø Strait toward Funen, Zealand and Jutland; exceptional sunsets
- **Note:** South island location means ~20km daily bike ride to reach north-island hiking; furthest from Nordby Bakker
- **Book:** vesborgfyr.dk (via Novasol)

## Activities

### Hiking

| Route | Distance | Difficulty | Highlights |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ballebjerg → Issehoved loop | ~11km | Easy–moderate | Cliffs, beach, Pelkeshøj views |
| Nordby full loop (via Møgelskår) | ~18km | Moderate | Glacial valleys, coast, village |
| Issehoved circuit | ~5km | Easy | Northernmost tip, sea views both sides |
| Ballebjerg path | ~5km | Easy–moderate | Highest point (64m), lookout tower, beach descent |
| Brattingsborg forest trail | ~varies | Easy | Fallow deer, forest streams, Lyushage lighthouse |
| Besser Rev coastal path | ~varies | Easy | Reef, birdlife, Kattegat coast |

**Tips:**
- Download Alltrails or pick up free hiking leaflets at VisitSamsø (Tranebjerg)
- Trails marked with colored poles; green triangles on the Ballebjerg–Issehoved route
- Book telebus (Samsø Bus +45 88 44 83 88) at least 2h in advance if needed for pickup — only 45 min notice required if arriving by ferry

### Cycling

| Route | Distance | Type |
|---|---|---|
| Full island loop | ~60km | Road/path |
| North island cultural route | ~62km, 28 sights | Signposted regional route |
| Sælvig → Nordby via Havvejen | ~16km | Scenic, hilly finish |
| South island relaxed loop | ~27km | Flat, villages, fields |

**Bike rental:**
- **Ø-Hjul** (e-bikes): self-service at Sælvig harbour; pre-book at oe-hjul.dk; range min. 65km; from DKK 08:00–22:00
- **Samsø Cykler**: regular + e-bikes delivered to harbour; also at Sælvig and Ballen; luggage transport available; samsøcykler.dk
- **Recommendation:** E-bikes for flexibility on longer hiking+cycling days; book before departure from Berlin
